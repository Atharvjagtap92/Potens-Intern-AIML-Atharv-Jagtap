# Potens Group Core Travel and Expense Policy (2026)

**Document ID**: `potens-core-travel`  
**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026  
**Scope**: All full-time employees of Potens IT Services and Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. (Core division)

---

## 1. Flight Travel Guidelines

### 1.1 Domestic Flights
All employees of the Core division must book Economy Class for all domestic flights (flights within India). Premium Economy and Business Class upgrades are not reimbursable for domestic routes under any circumstance.

### 1.2 International Flights
Business Class travel is strictly limited to international flights with a continuous flight duration exceeding 8 hours (excluding layovers). For flights under 8 hours, employees must fly Economy Class. Any exception to this rule requires written prior approval from the Vice President (VP) of HR.

---

## 2. Lodging and Accommodation

### 2.1 Hotel Capping
Reimbursement for hotel accommodation is capped based on city categorization:
- **Tier 1 Cities** (Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR): Maximum reimbursement is **$150 per night** (excluding taxes).
- **Tier 2/3 Cities** (Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and others): Maximum reimbursement is **$100 per night** (excluding taxes).

### 2.2 Shared Lodging
If two employees travel together, they are encouraged to share accommodation, in which case the budget cap is increased by 50% of the single occupancy limit.

---

## 3. Meal Allowances and Daily Per Diem

### 3.1 Per Diem Limits
Meal expenses are reimbursed based on a flat daily per diem allowance:
- **Tier 1 Cities** (Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR): Daily meal allowance is capped at **$50 per day**.
- **Other Cities**: Daily meal allowance is capped at **$35 per day**.

### 3.2 Reimbursement Exclusions
Alcoholic beverages are strictly excluded from company expense reimbursements. All meal claims must be backed by itemized receipts; lump-sum claims without receipts will be rejected.

---

## 4. Ground Transportation

### 4.1 Ride-sharing and Taxis
Standard ride-sharing services (e.g., Uber, Ola, or local taxi services) are approved for business commute. Premium or luxury class rides are not permitted.

### 4.2 Personal Vehicle Mileage
Employees using their personal vehicles for business travel are eligible for mileage reimbursement at a rate of **$0.45 per mile** (or equivalent in local currency). Prior manager authorization is required for trips exceeding 50 miles.

---

## 5. Approvals and Submission Timelines

### 5.1 Submission Window
All travel expense claims must be submitted via the Potens HR Portal within **30 days** of the travel end date. Claims submitted after 30 days will be rejected automatically.

### 5.2 Approval Matrix
- Claims under $500: Require direct Line Manager approval.
- Claims between $500 and $2,000: Require Line Manager and Department Head approval.
- Claims exceeding $2,000: Require CFO approval.
