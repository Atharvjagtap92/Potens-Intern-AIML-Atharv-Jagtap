# Potens Labs Travel and Expense Policy (2026)

**Document ID**: `potens-labs-travel`  
**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026  
**Scope**: All research scientists, engineers, and staff of Potens Labs (R&D Subsidiary)

---

## 1. Flight Travel Guidelines

### 1.1 Domestic Flights
Employees of Potens Labs are permitted to book Premium Economy Class for domestic flights (flights within India) if the scheduled flight duration exceeds 3 hours. For domestic flights under 3 hours, standard Economy Class must be booked.

### 1.2 International Flights
Business Class travel is permitted for international flights if the continuous flight duration exceeds 6 hours. For international flights under 6 hours, employees must book Premium Economy or Economy. Any exceptions require approval from the Vice President (VP) of R&D.

---

## 2. Lodging and Accommodation

### 2.1 Hotel Capping
Reimbursement for hotel accommodation is capped as follows:
- **Tier 1 Cities** (Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR, and international tech hubs): Maximum reimbursement is **$200 per night** (excluding taxes).
- **Tier 2/3 Cities**: Maximum reimbursement is **$150 per night** (excluding taxes).

---

## 3. Meal Allowances and Daily Per Diem

### 3.1 Per Diem Limits
Meal expenses are reimbursed based on a flat daily per diem:
- **Tier 1 Cities** (Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR, and international tech hubs): Daily meal allowance is capped at **$75 per day**.
- **Other Cities**: Daily meal allowance is capped at **$50 per day**.

### 3.2 Collaborator Dining and Alcohol
Unlike the core policy, moderate alcohol consumption is reimbursable up to **$15 per day** within the per diem limit, provided the meal is a dinner involving a research collaborator or external academic partner. Full receipts and names of attendees are mandatory for verification.

---

## 4. Ground Transportation

### 4.1 Ride-sharing and Taxis
Mid-tier ride-sharing options (e.g., Uber Comfort, Uber Premier, or Ola Prime) are pre-approved for business transit. Luxury or black-car services still require prior written permission.

### 4.2 Personal Vehicle Mileage
Employees using their personal vehicles for business travel are eligible for mileage reimbursement at a rate of **$0.55 per mile**.

---

## 5. Approvals and Submission Timelines

### 5.1 Submission Window
All travel expense claims must be submitted via the Labs Expense Tracker within **45 days** of the travel end date. Claims submitted after 45 days will require VP of R&D override approval.

### 5.2 Approval Matrix
- Claims under $1,000: Require Lab Manager approval.
- Claims between $1,000 and $3,000: Require Lab Manager and R&D Director approval.
- Claims exceeding $3,000: Require VP of R&D approval.
